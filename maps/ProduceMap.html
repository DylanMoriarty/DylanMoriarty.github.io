Project by Dylan Moriarty.

Data on food production was obtained from FAOSTAT, the Food and Agriculture Organization of the United Nations Statistcs Divison, and Wikipedia.

Data was drawn, projected, and presented through D3, JavaScript and the usual HTML/CSS. Map Projection used is Equirectangular.

This project produced for the UW-Madison course GEOG:575, with guidance from Robert Roth and Carl Sack.

The Font used for this page is Montserrat by Julieta Ulanovsky, which is provided here through Google Fonts.
