/ Blog / Icon Design

Recently I got a long list of icon requests from a mobile app developer for a series of "badges" the users can acquire. Here's how I took it on.

To start, I sent along a bunch of different badge types from Google Image Search to clarify whether they were looking for something more intricate and styled (A), or something simple and straightforward (C).

Along with that, I started working on what the inner design could be for each of the requested icons. More or less just brainstorming on paper.

I usually start with just some rough associations I personally make with each term- for sports various game balls, for example. If nothing is quite clicking there, I'll hop on Google Image Search and try to see what sort of things commonly come up on there, or consult online icon repositories like The Noun Project for inspiration. Even just asking friends what comes to mind when they think of the terms can be helpful.

With better direction established, I began to work on one badge to really nail down how the rest should look.

Following feedback on that, I went ahead and did rough finals of the rest of the set.

And here are the working finals!
